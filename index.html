Leedor

An EPUB reader written in Rust and compiled to WebAssembly.

Give it a try! Load a DRM-free eBook from your machine using the form above.

You can also click on one of these books to open it:

  • The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle (in English)
  • Don Quijote by Miguel de Cervantes (in Spanish)
  • Dom Casmurro by Machado de Assis (in Portuguese)
  • Chéri by Colette (in French)
  • Briefe aus dem Gefängnis by Rosa Luxemburg (in German)

Check out the source code on GitHub.
